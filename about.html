Hey! I’m Radhika.


I’m a Digital Product Designer from New Delhi who loves blending Behavioral Science and UX Design to create experiences that feel intuitive and human. My fascination with how people think and make decisions led me to pursue a Master’s in Behavioral Science, where I explored cognitive biases, decision-making, and the psychology behind great design.

Over the past few years, I’ve designed end-to-end workflows, crafted high-fidelity prototypes, and facilitated design reviews across startups, corporates, and design teams, ensuring products are both usable and delightful.

What I’ve been up to.
Recently, I had attented Behavioural science bootcamp by Irrational Labs online — where I immersed myself in behavioral design through their 3B Framework and its real-world product applications. I completed my Master’s with a 3.65/4 GPA.

Grateful for the people who shaped my path.
I’ve been fortunate to learn from incredible mentors and communities who’ve helped me merge psychology and design into meaningful, evidence-based work. Their guidance continues to inspire me to push the boundaries of what design can do for people.
